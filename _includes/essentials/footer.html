We are the ones we have been waiting for:

Keen to support the Pledge movement? 

The Pledge is an initiative of a group of Mindful Business Owners and General Managers in the tourism industry. As a community, we aim at finding practical and viable solutions to daily problems related to waste, energy, food-sourcing, staff training and customer relationships. We take positive actions to solve key environmental and social issues.

Bali and Beyond
We aim at inspiring a shift from mass-tourism to travel with intention, in Bali first, throughout Indonesia next and in the World ultimately.
