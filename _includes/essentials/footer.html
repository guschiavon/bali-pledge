This Pledge for Bali is a BGreener initiative.

BGreener is the hand that propels tourism businesses in solving social and environmental management challenges in their communities.

Together, owners and managers learn and share with each other how to find practical and viable solutions to daily problems related to waste, energy, food-sourcing, staff training, customer relationships. Members help grow each other's businesses, support one another in the daily operations. Members meet together to create community, to network and to take positive actions to solve key environmental and social issues.
