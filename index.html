The Pledge for Bali

 With the cessation of worldwide travel, now is the time to reimagine the future of Bali, an island whose culture and environment have been under tremendous strain from mass tourism.  With your Pledge and that of thousands of others we hope to strengthen Balinese traditions and protect its beauty for us and future generations.

We Choose To Be The Change We Wish To See In Bali.

Sign the Pledge
{% include ongkara.svg %}

This Pledge is inspired by the ancient Balinese philosophy of Tri Hita Karana whereby each person seeks to live in harmony with their Community, Spirituality, and Environment. Philosophy rooted in the belief that by striving to make tangible, positive impacts in all three components of Tri Hita Karana, we achieve a sense of wellness, harmony, and peace. 

In Order For One To Be Well, We Must All Be Well.



Tri Hita Karana - We Are One

Honor The SPIRIT OF BALI 

I am part of Mother Universe and respect the harmony and balance with Her, provided by Bali's spiritual practices. The powerful rituals held by Balinese will be honored in our interactions, our life at home and in the streets. We are the universe.

{% assign path = page.second_highlight_image %} {% assign alt = page.second_highlight_image_alt_text %} {% include image.html path = page.highlight_image alt = page.highlight_image_alt_text %}

Stay Close To NATURE

I am part of Mother Nature. The air I breathe, the water I drink, and the food I savour are the keys to our future. If we continue to take every single part of nature without giving back, it will be drained and this will harm life. We are nature, we are our environments.

{% assign path = page.third_highlight_image %} {% assign alt = page.third_highlight_image_alt_text %} {% include image.html path = page.highlight_image alt = page.highlight_image_alt_text %}

Be The COMMUNITY You Want To Have

We are essentially emotional and social creatures. I cannot thrive without others thriving too, so FOR ME TO BE WELL, WE ALL NEED TO BE WELL.  In supporting one another, we are supporting the whole world.  We need harmonious relationships; we want to give. We are one.

I am in Bali because I want to be well.

This is my pledge, to be well I pledge to:

Protect Bali's natural resources and environment.

Be in harmony with my local community.

Respect the cultural and spiritual essence of Bali.

To be well, we all need to be well.

We are one.
