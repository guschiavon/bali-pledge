The Pledge for Bali

In order to be well, we all need to be well. Inspired by Balinese spirit, community and environment, we pledge to commit to taking steps towards reducing more negative environmental and social footprints. Instead, we will make a tangible positive one.

{% include ongkara.svg %}

A clear and agreed-upon set of values is essential to accomplishing clear goals. The pledge offers an overview of such values, that we consider essential parts of helping us create positive global change for the benefit of all people and all life on Bali, and on this planet.



Tri Hita Karana - We Are One

The plege honours the Balinese Tri Hita Karana philosophy and is supported by its three pillars:

{% assign path = page.first_highlight_image %} {% assign alt = page.first_highlight_image_alt_text %} {% include image.html path = page.highlight_image alt = page.highlight_image_alt_text %}

Parahyangan

Honour the spirit of Bali.

"I am part of Mother Universe and respect the protection of harmony and balance with Her, provided by the spiritual practices on Bali. The wonderful rituals held by Balinese will be honored in our interactions, our life at home and in the streets. We are the universe."

{% assign path = page.second_highlight_image %} {% assign alt = page.second_highlight_image_alt_text %} {% include image.html path = page.highlight_image alt = page.highlight_image_alt_text %}

Palemahan

Stay close to nature. 

"I am part of Mother Nature. The air I breathe, the water I drink, or the food I savour is key to my future. If I continue to take every single part of nature without giving back, it will be drained and this will harm life. We are nature, we are our environments."

{% assign path = page.third_highlight_image %} {% assign alt = page.third_highlight_image_alt_text %} {% include image.html path = page.highlight_image alt = page.highlight_image_alt_text %}

Pawongan

Be the community you want to have.

"We are essentially social creatures. I cannot thrive without others thriving too, so if I want to be well - we all need to be well. In hurting one another, we are hurting ourselves. We want harmonious relationships, we want to give. We are one."

I am in Bali because I want to be well.

This is my pledge, to be well I pledge to:

Respect the cultural and spiritual essence of Bali.

Protect Bali's natural resources and environment.

Be in harmony with my local community.

To be well, we all need to be well.

We are one.

This Pledge for Bali is a BGreener initiative.

BGreener is the hand that propels tourism businesses in solving social and environmental management challenges in their communities.

Together, owners and managers learn and share with each other how to find practical and viable solutions to daily problems related to waste, energy, food-sourcing, staff training, customer relationships. Members help grow each other's businesses, support one another in the daily operations. Members meet together to create community, to network and to take positive actions to solve key environmental and social issues.
